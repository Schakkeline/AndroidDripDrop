Hello There

This is Mami Wata

Nice that you are here - and welcome to the Showreel Website for my App! This App reminds you, that you should drink enough water during the day - and if you don't comply, it teaches you by donating small amounts of money. This is just a theoretical App - everything is set up but not available as an acutal App ... and it's still a work in progress ;)

About Me

My name is Jacqueline and I designed and developed this project as part of my studies "Digital Media Design" at the University of Applied Sciences Ravensburg-Weingarten. This app concept and development took place in the Android Development course.

Why Mami Wata

I always forget to drink enough - especially when I am working on projects or do something I really enjoy. I downloaded a lot of Apps that should remind me to drink water, but I always ignored them or deleted them after a while. So I decided to design and develop my own Water-Reminder App with a little twist.

Benefits

If I don't drink the water, why not donate it to people in need? This is the intention behind the Mami Wata project. There are so many peope in the world with no access to clean water, so why not teach yourself a good habit and donate a small amount this way? The source code is also available on GitHub - maybe someone can use parts on their own projects.

Design and Development

This is my first project with Android - so it was my intention to try as many different things and functions as possible. I wanted to try fragments, learn how to bring my design to life with XML, take my first steps with Firebase and learn how everything works together to build the skeleton for an App.
